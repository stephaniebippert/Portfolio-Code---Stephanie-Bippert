About Stephanie

I’m a hard working, detail oriented, creative, and analytical problem solver with a passion for learning, leadership, and taking on new challenges. I have a Bachelors Degree in Digital Marketing from the University of Georgia (Go Dawgs), 3+ years of work experience, and 3 certifications. As Head of Marketing at Kaizen Analytix, a data analytics firm, and a freelance UX/UI Designer, my sweet spot lies at the intersection of Business / Marketing, Analytics, and Design.
